Zero Waste

At Second Slice Cafe, we are proud of our 100% Zero Waste policy.

All of our ingredients, including our organic coffee beans, are sourced from local and sustainable suppliers.

We trace all of our ingredients to their origin, and ensure that we use everything possible.

Our cups are 100% recyclable.

All waste, including coffee grounds, are used or donated, and often finds another life as fertiliser.

In a time of changing climate, maintaining a policy of Zero Waste has never been so important.
